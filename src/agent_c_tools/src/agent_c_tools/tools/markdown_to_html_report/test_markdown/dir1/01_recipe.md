# Simple Pancake Recipe

## Ingredients
- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder

## Instructions
1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
2. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth
3. Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes per side
4. Serve hot with syrup

*Makes about 6 pancakes*
